Points

Points are awarded for the following accomplishments:

  • Correct prediction for a game:
    • Correct Sign(1/X/2) - {{groupPhase[0]?.signPoints}} points in the group phase; {{knockoutPhase[0]?.signPoints}} points in the knockout phase.
    • Correct Result - {{groupPhase[0]?.scorePoints}} points in the group phase; {{knockoutPhase[0]?.scorePoints}} points in the knockout phase.
    • Team progressing to next stage (applicable only for elimination games, which end in draw) - {{knockoutPhase[0]?.progressPoints}} points.
  • Correct prediction for the champion of the tournament - the number of awarded points is based on the date of placing the prediction. If placed before the start of the first game, you get the maximum 32 points. If placed on a later date, you get a number of points corresponding to the remaining days of the torunament. You can change your prediction as many times as needed before the start of the final game, only the latest one will count.
  • Correct prediction for the top scorer of the tournament. Points are calculated in the same manner as for the champion. If there are several players with equal number of scored goals, all of them will count as correct predictions (assists or other statistics will not be used to determine a single top scorer). Goals scored in a penalty shootout and own goals do not count.
Leagues

Upon registration every user becomes a member of the "Overall" league. You can create a private league to contest only against your friends and colleagues. Private leagues are managed by their creators. You can join a league if you were provided with its name and access code.

Prizes

You can win a portion of the money in the bank only if you have contributed yourself. The entry fee for the "Overall" league is 10 BGN and it should be submitted before the start of the tournament. The money is awarded to the 3 players who have paid the entry fee and acummulated the most points in the following manner - 1st place - 50%, 2nd place - 30%, 3rd place - 20%. If two or more players finish with equal points, the prize portions are split accordingly. You can participate in the game and appear in the standings without paying any money - in this case your points will be marked in red - if you do well, your prize will be honor.

The entry fee amount and distribution of the money in the bank, if any, for a private league should be agreed upon by its members - there are no restrictions imposed by this website.
